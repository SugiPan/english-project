Shakuntala: A Character Study

“Marked by love, forgotten by fate — the tale of Śakuntalā.”

By: Sugyan Panigrahi, Sujit Kumar Yadav, Sushant Kumar, Paranjay Gupta

Introduction:

In the hush of ancient groves, where sages whispered wisdom to the winds, bloomed a tale not just of love — but of loss, longing, and divine memory. Śakuntalā is no mere character etched in time; she is a melody of emotions, a dance of destiny, a flame both tender and tenacious. Her story, nestled in the heart of Kālidāsa’s immortal verses, is at once a lament and a lullaby — the kind that echoes across generations.
